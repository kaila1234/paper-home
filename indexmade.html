Construction Paper

Construction paper is made from wood pulp, which is dyed and pressed to create sheets of paper in various colors. Because of the durability and rough feel of construction paper, it is used for art and coloring.


Tissue Paper

Tissue paper is made from recycled paper pulp that is processed to create a thin, soft paper product. Because of the soft and thin feel of tissue paper, it is mainly used in the bathroom.


Parchment Paper

Parchment paper is made by treating paper pulp with sulfuric acid to create a non-stick, heat-resistant surface. Because of the non-stick of parchment paper, it is mainly used for baking.
