Construction Paper

Construction paper is made from wood pulp, which is dyed and pressed to create sheets of paper in various colors.

Tissue Paper

Tissue paper is made from recycled paper pulp that is processed to create a thin, soft paper product.

Parchment Paper

Parchment paper is made by treating paper pulp with sulfuric acid to create a non-stick, heat-resistant surface.
