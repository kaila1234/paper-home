High Consumption

Americans use a massive amount of paper annually, with estimates reaching over 90 million short tons of paper and paperboard each year. The average American uses 700 pounds of paper yearly, maing the United States the biggest consumer of paper.



How Many Trees it Takes

It is estimated that a standard pine tree, with 45ft of the usable trunk and a diameter of eight inches, will produce around 10,000 sheets of paper. In another way, one ream of paper (which is 500 sheets) will use 5% of a tree.



Water Usage

When it comes to making paper, water is required. It uses a large amount of water to make a single stack of papers; 10 liters. Water is needed to smmothen and suspend the fibers of the paper to make it more durable.
